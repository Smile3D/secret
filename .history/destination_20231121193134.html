Wellington Airport is the main international airfield situated in the suburb of Rongotai, in Wellington. It is the third busiest airport in the country as it handles around 7 million travelers per year. Wellington Airport is also the main hub for Air New Zealand. It offers a wide array of domestic and international flights to more than 50 countries worldwide.

Wellington Airport is located 7 km away from the city center. However, one can access it only by road as it lies in the southern end of the North Island Section of State Highway 1. If you need to get to the city center by public transport, mind that only busses are available as Wellington Airport is located far from the local railway station.
