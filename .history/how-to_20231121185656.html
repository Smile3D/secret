How do I get from Zurich Airport to Basel by bus?

The cheapest way to get from Zurich Airport (ZRH) to Basel is Flixbus Bus, but nowadays due to the COVID-19 epidemic situation direct bus routes from Zurich Airport to Basel are limited or canceled. Now you can take Flixbus Bus 33 at the bus stop of the airport to get to Zurich Bus Station. The price of a ticket is about €10. The final stop is Zurich Bus Station. The travel time is about 22 minutes. Then you need to have about a 7-minutes walk (590 meters) to get Zurich HB, the Main Railway Station and continue your trip to Basel by train. You can buy bus tickets in advance on Flixbus.

Travel Tip: You can save €12 by buying train tickets from Zurich to Basel in advance. In case of early booking, a boarding pass for an adult passenger costs €18 instead of €30.

How do I get from Zurich Airport to Basel by train?

The second cheapest way to get from Zurich Airport (ZRH) to Basel is a train. You can take the Swiss Railways train directly from Zurich Airport to Basel. Zürich Flughafen Train Station is located directly outside the airport. The terminal station in Basel is Basel SBB. Travel time is 1h 28m. The total distance is 98,1 km. Trains run hourly.

How much are train tickets from Zurich Airport to Basel?

The price of a ticket price for adult passengers is €30 – 60. You can buy a boarding pass for a train from Zurich Airport at the ticket offices at the station. The vending machines accept cash and credit cards. You can find the train schedule for the route Zurich Airport – Basel on the official website of the Swiss Railway Transport.

Travel Tip: You can save €12 by buying train tickets from Zurich to Basel in advance. In case of early booking, a boarding pass for an adult passenger costs €18 instead of €30.

Keep in mind that there are penalties for stowaway travel on Swiss trains. Do not throw away the ticket until the end of the trip.
